What is this website?

This is the creator speaking, Farid Wakim. This is a website I created because I feel there aren't enough websites these days that do this. "Do what?" you may ask, well this site is a website that wants to explain how EVERYTHING works to you, and by that, we mean everything linked to the latest discoveries and creations of mankind. This website was created for 2 reasons: first, I had to submit a project for my Web Development Static course, and second, most importantly, I am a very curious person by heart. I've disassembled more things than I can remember, I've asked too many questions, and I've always had a nack for wondering how things functions. I will explain how everything started, how it developped, and how it is today. Now, I know that what I'm attempting to do, isn't easy to do; this is where you come in: if you also have the curiosity to find how things work, Click on the "Submit/Ask/Help" button in order to:

  • Submit Ideas On What To Cover Next
  • Ask any question you might have about a certain topic, or about me
  • Help out if you feel like you could help out in some way or another
